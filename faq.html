FAQs

Are your cat collars safe for everyday use?

Yes, our cat collars are thoughtfully designed for daily wear. We use soft, lightweight, and pet-safe materials that feel comfortable around your cat’s neck. Each collar is carefully tested to ensure it does not cause irritation, discomfort, or restrict movement, allowing your cat to play, rest, and explore freely throughout the day.

Do your collars include a safety breakaway feature?

Absolutely. Safety is our top priority. All our collars come with a breakaway buckle that automatically releases under pressure if your cat gets caught on something. This feature helps prevent choking or injury, giving you peace of mind while your cat explores both indoors and outdoors.

What sizes do your cat collars come in?

Our collars are fully adjustable and designed to fit most cats comfortably, from playful kittens to fully grown adults. Each product page includes clear sizing guidelines so you can choose the perfect fit. We recommend leaving a small gap of two fingers between the collar and your cat’s neck for maximum comfort and safety.

Can the collars be washed or cleaned?

Yes, our cat collars are easy to maintain. For best results, gently hand-wash the collar using mild soap and lukewarm water, then allow it to air dry completely. Regular cleaning helps keep the collar fresh, hygienic, and looking new for longer.

Are your collars suitable for indoor and outdoor cats?

Our collars are designed for both indoor and outdoor cats. They are durable enough to handle outdoor adventures while remaining soft and comfortable for indoor lounging. The secure yet safe design ensures your cat stays stylish and protected wherever they go.
